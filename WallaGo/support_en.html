📌 About the App

WallaGo is a two-player wall Go game that requires strategic thinking and creativity. Play in real time with your friend or against AI, and use walls to expand your territory and claim victory!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q. Google Sign-In is not working
    Please check your Google account settings and network connection, then try again.
  • Q. I can't match with a friend
    Make sure your friend has entered the exact same room code.
  • Q. My score or ranking looks incorrect
    Please fully close and reopen the app, or check your network. If you are using social login, try logging out and back in.
